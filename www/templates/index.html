Manage related secrets in Vaults

Vaults help you collect secrets by projects or environments. A central repository for encrypted secrets is a cybersecurity best practice.

Vault makes it easy to share secrets with your team and audit secrets usage. The Cloak user interface manages end to end encryption and decryption in the browser.

Manage access with Service Accounts

Service accounts let team members or applications access secrets from Vaults. The user interface generates a private key which only the user can see. Deploy the key to your local development environment or your infrastructure.

Revoking a service account removes access to the secrets.

The Cloak CLI gives developers easy access to secrets

Cloak injects your secrets into your application as environment variables.

The CLI tool has a minimal footprint and runs as well on a laptop as in a docker container.
